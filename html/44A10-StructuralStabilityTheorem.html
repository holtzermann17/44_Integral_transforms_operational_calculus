Structural stability theorem


Let M be a compact differentiable manifold and let f:M→M be a Cr diffeomorphism.

Then, f is structurally stable in the C1 topology if and only if it is Axiom A and satisfies the strong transversality condition. The question of knowing if this is valid for the Cr topology, r≥2 is still open.
